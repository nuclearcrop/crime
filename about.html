About CrimeLens India

CrimeLens India is a public crime analytics and transparency platform designed to help citizens explore crime statistics, scam trends, police performance, public safety indicators and government transparency data through interactive dashboards and reports.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make publicly available crime and policing data easier to understand through modern analytics and visualizations. CrimeLens aims to:
  • Improve public awareness.
  • Increase transparency in policing data.
  • Provide state and district level insights.
  • Track scam and cybercrime trends.
  • Make public datasets easier to access.
  • Encourage data-driven discussions on public safety.

Methodology

CrimeLens aggregates publicly available information from government sources and presents it through dashboards and analytics tools. Data may be updated periodically depending on source availability and update frequency. Statistics shown on the platform are intended for informational purposes and should not be treated as official legal records.

Disclaimer

CrimeLens does not create, modify or verify official government records. The platform only displays publicly available information obtained from government and public sources. Users should always verify critical information directly from the respective government department or official authority. Private, restricted or confidential records are not displayed.
